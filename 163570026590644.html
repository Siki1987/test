Current openings

You can make a significant contribution to an organisation that is determined to make lasting change in the lives of children

Explore our careers page to learn more about our teams , what we value in our employees and what it's like to work for us . 

Our people are ambitious, creative and outspoken. We run programs for children, young people and adults in Australia and overseas. We stand up for children's rights. We're here because we want all children to be educated, healthy and live a life free from violence. But we can't do all of this without great people.  Apply now .

A little bit more about us

Within Save the Children, our teams work collectively to change the lives of children and families in need, in Australia and overseas. We are proud that our staff are made up of talented people from a great diversity of backgrounds and experiences; each and every one of us passionate and dedicated to go the extra mile.

Our employee benefits

We want to make working at Save the Children rewarding for you, both personally and professionally. We’re a committed, hard-working team. And we work on issues that are often challenging and require resilience and strength of spirit. But we also recognise the importance of self -care and the right to enjoy a balanced life. That’s why we offer a variety of benefits to help make your career with us more fulfilling, enjoyable and sustainable.
